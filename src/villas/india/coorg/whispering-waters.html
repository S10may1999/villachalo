Escape to the beauty of a lush tropical forest at Whispering Waters in Coorg, with modern comfort in an architectural gem of a bungalow. Come here for a combination of unique experiences to thrill all your senses… At the Bungalow location: breathtaking vistas of hills and rivers, Kodava culinary cruises, plantation safaris, ayurvedic massages and riverside picnics…In the vicinity: hill station golfing, naturalist guided reserve forest trails, places of historical and religious significance, and shopping for Coorg food stuffs, Jewellery. Nestled within the Hematala estate, spanning 25 acres of coffee and spice plantations, lies the captivating 'Whispering Waters' bungalow. Blending Coorg's plantation-style charm with contemporary design, this architectural gem offers a fusion of comfort and authenticity. Admire the colonial teak and cane furniture, the red roof tiles, and the modern amenities that enhance your stay. With floor-to-ceiling glass windows and balconies, every corner provides panoramic views of the surrounding natural wonders….early morning mists rising, sunsets over hills, perpetual gurgling sounds of the Harangi river, and the refreshing monsoon. At the Hematala estate, embark on enchanting plantation walks, where Planters unveil the secrets of Coorg's renowned coffee and spice crops, and picnic at the bottom of the hill by the Harangi river, a tributary of the sacred Cauvery river. Let your taste buds embark on a gastronomic journey that reflects the essence of Coorg's rich culinary heritage at Whispering Waters. Our chefs offer unique coorg cuisine ranging from kodava pork, to wild bamboo shoots & mushrooms, accompanying coorg breads ( akkirottis and kadambuttus), to avocado salads to passion fruit juices, skilfully preparing each dish using freshly forest foraged local ingredients and native aromatic spices. Our Chef can supplement this with tantalizing Tibetan meals and flavorful Kerala cuisine, crafted by local chefs. End this with leisurely, savouring a full barista experience of six different ways of preparing the famous coorg coffee. And maybe an ayurvedic massage, after. Conveniently located just 10 kilometers from Madikeri town, Whispering Waters offers easy access to a myriad of activities. Indulge in thrilling treks through Coorg's pristine hills where the Pandava’s are reputed to have travelled ( Kota bette), explore the Pushpagiri reserve forest with a naturalist to sight endangered species, picnic at Dubare elephant camp by the Cauvery, explore historical and religious sites, golf at century plus Mercara Downs golf course, or immerse yourself in the vibrant city life of local shopping and restaurants. Return to the tranquility of the villa, where cherished memories are made against the backdrop of nature's captivating beauty. Uncover the essence of Coorg at Whispering Waters. Are you ready to embark on an unforgettable retreat that will leave an indelible mark on your soul?
