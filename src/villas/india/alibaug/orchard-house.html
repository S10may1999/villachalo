Nestled just a short 20-minute journey from Mandwa Jetty, the exquisite Orchard House emerges from the verdant embrace of Alibaug's landscapes. At the Orchard, a symphony of nature and architecture unfolds seamlessly. The ground floor welcomes you with grace, housing four elegantly adorned bedrooms, a semi-open dining area that beckons for shared feasts, and outdoor lounges where laughter and stories find their stage. Step in through the doors, and it’s like you’re transported into the pages of an architectural magazine, where earthy tones dance with modern accents. Every element has been meticulously curated.This home is also a true retreat for avid gym enthusiasts, with a fully functional gym to maintain your fitness amidst this tranquil escape. Ascend to the upper floor, and a private world of opulence awaits – an enchanting private pool, a deck that invites you to bask under the sun's gentle caress, and a living area that cradles conversations and togetherness. In every season, the Orchard House offers respite. Beyond its walls, Alibaug beckons – its beaches, villages, and hidden treasures awaiting your exploration. Delight in homestyle culinary delights or savor the comforts of Mumbai delivered to your door. Orchard House is Indeed, a retreat that inspires. An unparalleled escape, a pause from the relentless cadence of daily life.
