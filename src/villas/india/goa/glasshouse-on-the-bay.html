A one-of-a-kind home, built right on the edge of the water. Welcome to Glasshouse On The Bay. Inviting abundant natural light, the large glass facade offers magnificent views of the sea from every room. Ideally suited for small groups, this contemporary two-storied home comprises three ensuite bedrooms, two living areas, a dining area and a reading room. Outdoors, you can take in the stunning vista from the large deck, the balcony and the pool. Spend hours catching up on your reading list, enjoy al fresco style meals and reconnect with yourself and your loved ones in the serene environment. Situated in Chapora, the home allows easy access to North Goa's beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife. Head to Ashvem or Mandrem for a refreshing dip in the sea, catch the sunset from atop Chapora Fort and visit the bustling markets before embarking on a splendid culinary journey in the quaint village of Assagaon. Whether you’re looking for adventure or rejuvenation, Lohono’s Goa makes the perfect getaway.
